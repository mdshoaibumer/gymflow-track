# GymFlow Track — AI Video Generation Prompts

## Provider Recommendations
- **Premium:** Google Veo 2 / Seedance 2.0 (via fal.ai) — Best cinematic motion, consistent characters
- **Mid-tier:** Kling v2.1 (via fal.ai) — Good motion, affordable
- **Budget:** MiniMax Video (via fal.ai) — Acceptable quality at lowest cost
- **Free:** Image-based animation via Remotion (Ken Burns, parallax, crossfade)

## Video Specifications
- **Duration per clip:** 4-6 seconds (provider default)
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum
- **Frame Rate:** 24fps (cinematic)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
