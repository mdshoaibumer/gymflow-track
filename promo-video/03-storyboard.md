## Transition Guide

| From → To | Transition Type | Duration |
|-----------|----------------|----------|
| Scene 1 → 2 | Hard cut to black, then fade up on laptop | 0.5s |
| Scene 2 → 3 | Smooth zoom into dashboard screen | 0.3s |
| Scene 3 → 4 | Match cut (screen → physical space) | 0.2s |
| Scene 4 → 5 | Cross dissolve | 0.4s |
| Scene 5 → 6 | Wipe (screen content pushes in) | 0.3s |
| Scene 6 → 7 | Smooth zoom out to reveal full dashboard | 0.3s |
| Scene 7 → 8 | Dissolve to golden hour | 0.5s |
| Scene 8 → End | Fade to black → Logo | 0.8s |
