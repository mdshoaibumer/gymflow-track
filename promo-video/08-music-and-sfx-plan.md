### Scene-by-Scene Music Direction

#### Scene 1: The Problem (0:00–0:18)
```
Energy: 2-3
Instruments: Low ambient pad, subtle tension drone, isolated piano notes
Mood: Melancholic, isolated, weighted
Notes: Sparse. Let silence do the work. Single piano notes spaced far apart.
       Low sub-bass drone at ~60Hz provides unease.
       No rhythm section. No percussion.
```

#### Scene 2: The Reveal (0:18–0:30)
```
Energy: 4-5
Instruments: Piano melody emerges, warm synth pad, first subtle pulse
Mood: Hope, discovery, possibility
Notes: KEY CHANGE from minor to major at 0:18.
       Piano plays a simple ascending 4-note motif (the "GymFlow theme").
       Warm analog synth pad swells underneath.
       First hint of a pulse/kick at 0:25 (quarter notes, very soft).
       This is the "sunrise" moment in the music.
```

#### Scene 3: Member Management (0:30–0:42)
```
Energy: 5-6
Instruments: Piano motif continues, add light percussion (shaker, hi-hat), bass synth
Mood: Organized, flowing, productive
Notes: Rhythm section enters — light and driving but not aggressive.
       Hi-hat pattern: eighth notes at 50% velocity.
       Bass synth: simple root notes following chord progression.
       Piano motif repeats but adds harmony.
```

#### Scene 4: QR Check-In (0:42–0:52)
```
Energy: 6-7
Instruments: Full light percussion, synth arpeggios, bass
Mood: Modern, technological, seamless
Notes: Add a subtle synth arpeggio (16th notes, filtered).
       Represents the digital/technological nature of QR scanning.
       Snare enters — soft, electronic.
       Energy lifts but stays controlled.
```

#### Scene 5: Trainer Management (0:52–1:02)
```
Energy: 7
Instruments: Full band, strings hint, warm electric piano
Mood: Human, motivational, warm
Notes: Brief moment of warmth — electric piano replaces some synth.
       Could add subtle string pad for emotional depth.
       This is the "human moment" in an otherwise tech-forward track.
       Percussion continues but pattern varies (add fills).
```

#### Scene 6: Payments & Revenue (1:02–1:14)
```
Energy: 7-8
Instruments: Driving bass, full percussion, ascending synth lines
Mood: Growth, momentum, building power
Notes: Bass becomes more prominent and driving.
       Ascending synth lines mirror the chart growth visuals.
       Add subtle sidechain compression on pads for rhythmic pump.
       Build anticipation for the dashboard climax.
```

#### Scene 7: Business Dashboard (1:14–1:24)
```
Energy: 8-9
Instruments: Full arrangement, orchestral hits, powerful pad
Mood: Authority, complete picture, peak confidence
Notes: Biggest moment before the finale.
       Add a single orchestral hit on the downbeat of Scene 7.
       Full frequency spectrum engaged.
       This is the "everything comes together" moment.
```

#### Scene 8: The Close (1:24–1:30)
```
Energy: 9-10 → resolve to 5
Instruments: Full crescendo → piano + pad resolve
Mood: Triumph → settled confidence → brand moment
Notes: 0:00–0:04 of scene: Full crescendo. All elements playing.
       0:04–0:06: Elements drop away. Just piano + warm pad.
       Final chord: Dmaj9 — open, resolved, confident.
       Brand chime at logo reveal: single bell/chime tone (D5).
       2 seconds of silence/very quiet pad to close.
```

## AUDIO MIX SPECIFICATIONS

### Levels
| Element | Target Level | Pan |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| Voiceover | -6 dBFS (peak) | Center |
| Music (under VO) | -18 to -22 dBFS | Stereo wide |
| Music (no VO) | -12 to -14 dBFS | Stereo wide |
| SFX | -18 to -24 dBFS | Positioned |
| Ambient | -30 to -36 dBFS | Stereo wide |

### Processing Chain
1. **Voiceover:** De-ess → EQ (presence boost 3-5kHz) → Compression (3:1, -18dB threshold) → Subtle room reverb (0.8s decay, 15% wet)
2. **Music:** Sidechain compression triggered by VO (-4dB reduction when VO active) → Stereo widening → Limiter
3. **SFX:** EQ to fit in mix → Spatial positioning → Level automation
4. **Master:** Loudness normalization to -14 LUFS (YouTube standard) → True peak limiter at -1 dBTP

| Deliverable | Format | Sample Rate | Bit Depth |
|-------------|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Production master | WAV | 48kHz | 24-bit |
| YouTube delivery | AAC | 48kHz | 320kbps |
| Social media | AAC | 44.1kHz | 256kbps |
